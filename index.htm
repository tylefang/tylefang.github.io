Biography

Tao Fang is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Digital Intelligent Language Services at Macao Millennium College. Public academic profiles list his PhD affiliation as the University of Macau and identify his research areas as natural language processing, large language models, machine translation, grammatical error correction, and nonlinear integrable systems.

His recent work studies the evaluation and improvement of large language models for grammatical error correction, writing assistance, dialogue summarization, tool-calling agents, hallucination reduction in text generation, and domain-specific text classification.
